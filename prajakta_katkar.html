The Journey of Prajakta Katkar

Prajakta Katkar has been practicing part-time natural farming for the past 2–3 years while also working in an MNC company. Despite her busy schedule, she continues farming as both a passion and a responsibility towards society.

Embracing Natural Farming

Since childhood, she has been aware of the harmful effects of chemical farming, which inspired her to adopt natural farming. She uses natural fertilizers prepared from cow dung and organic materials to ensure safe, chemical-free produce.

Achievements and Techniques

Prajakta focuses on producing food that is safe for consumption and nutritious. Her main products include nachani flour, jowar flour, bajra flour, rava, and other flours. She manages farming on weekends, balancing it with her professional career.

  • Balancing Career and Farming: Works in an MNC while practicing part-time natural farming for 2–3 years.
  • Natural Practices: Uses cow dung and organic materials as fertilizers to ensure safe, chemical-free food.
  • Products: Sells nachani, jowar, bajra flour, and rava directly in city areas like Kothrud.
  • Message to Youth: Encourages youth to support farming, promote healthy food, and stay connected to their roots.

Inspiring Others

She sells her products in Kothrud city, directly connecting farmers and consumers. Her message to youth is that even if they work in cities, they can support farming and promote healthy food systems without forgetting their roots.
