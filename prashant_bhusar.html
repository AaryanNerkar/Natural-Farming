The Journey of Prashant Bhusar

Prashant Bhusar, a natural farmer from Sangamner, started his farming journey in 2015 with a background in Mechanical Engineering. He now has 10 years of experience in the field.

Embracing Natural Farming

Mr. Bhusar owns 232 acres of land, where he practices organic farming that's centered around cows. He also runs a dairy farm with about 200 desi cows. This method of farming has helped improve the organic carbon content of the soil, making it healthier and reducing the need for pesticides.

Achievements and Techniques

Prashant Bhusar has earned organic certification for his farm. He practices multi-cropping, growing a variety of crops like wheat, vegetables, and pulses simultaneously. Additionally, he produces different types of oils, including sunflower oil and peanut oil. His produce is also exported to other countries, thanks to his Food Security Licence and international certifications.

Inspiring Others

Beyond his farming, Mr. Bhusar actively promotes a healthy lifestyle.
