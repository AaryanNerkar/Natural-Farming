The Journey of Vaman Dada

Mr. Vaman Dada from Narayangram has dedicated the last nine years to natural farming. After completing his service at Tata Motors, he returned to his roots with a mission to provide society with healthy, chemical-free food.

Embracing Natural Farming

On his 30 gunthe land, he focuses on sugarcane cultivation using sustainable and chemical-free practices. His farming ensures soil health and promotes natural methods of food production.

Achievements and Techniques

From his sugarcane, he prepares value-added products like gulachi kakvi (liquid jaggery) and gul powder, both made without chemicals. These products are pure, nutritious, and eco-friendly, offering a healthier alternative to refined sugar.

  • Farming Journey: Left his Tata Motors career to pursue natural farming for the last nine years in Narayangram.
  • Sustainable Practices: Cultivates sugarcane on 30 gunthe land using chemical-free, eco-friendly methods.
  • Value-added Products: Produces gulachi kakvi (liquid jaggery) and gul powder as healthy alternatives to refined sugar.
  • Inspiration: Demonstrates how one can successfully shift from corporate life to natural farming for health and sustainability.

Inspiring Others

His successful shift from a corporate career to natural farming sets an example for others. His journey proves that sustainable farming not only benefits health and the environment but also revives traditional food practices.
