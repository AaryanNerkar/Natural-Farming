The Journey of Jyotsana Chaudhari

Jyotsna Chaudhuri began her natural farming journey 8–9 years ago with the belief that farming should focus on community support and health. She emphasizes that natural farming is not just a method but a way to bring purity and wellness back to society.

Embracing Natural Farming

She avoids chemical fertilizers and instead uses cow dung–based fertilizers and organic mixtures. Her focus extends to clean milk production, feeding cows with organic fodder to provide pure, healthy, and safe milk, especially for children and the elderly.

Achievements and Techniques

Her main products include buttermilk, milk, paneer, ghee, and other milk-based items, all prepared without chemicals or preservatives. These products retain natural taste and nutrition, offering safe alternatives to chemically adulterated market options.

  • Farming Journey: Started 8–9 years ago with a vision of community support, purity, and health through natural farming.
  • Clean Milk Focus: Produces pure milk and products like paneer, ghee, and buttermilk using organic fodder and natural methods.
  • Natural Practices: Uses cow dung–based fertilizers and organic mixtures to maintain soil fertility and chemical-free crops.
  • Message to Youth: Encourages young people to join natural farming with innovation and technology for a healthier future.

Inspiring Others

Her motto is that farming should not only be about profit but also about community, good health, and sustainable growth. She encourages youth to adopt natural farming, bringing new ideas, technology, and awareness to ensure food security and a healthier society.
